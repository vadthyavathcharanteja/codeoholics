Who We Are

CodeOholics is a vibrant student-led community at CMR Technical Campus dedicated to coding, technology, and innovation. We organize workshops, hackathons, and events that bring together students passionate about learning and problem-solving.

Our mission is to nurture talent, foster creativity, and prepare students for real-world challenges in tech and innovation.

Our Mission

Empowering students to innovate, collaborate, and excel in the digital era.

Innovation

Collaboration

Growth

Helping members reach their full potential in tech careers.
